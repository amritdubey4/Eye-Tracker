This is a sample article to track your eye movements. Please calibrate the system first using the button above. Once calibrated, read this text naturally. The system will record where your eyes linger.

How it works: WebGazer.js uses your webcam to detect your pupil position. It maps this position to screen coordinates. We store these coordinates in a local array. When you click "Generate Heatmap", we use heatmap.js to visualize the density of these points.
